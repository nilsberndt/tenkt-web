ABOUT

10kT is a dynamic, real-time text editing platform where {wordblock}s can be modified by users from the Android app and viewed live on the web at 10kt.app. Changes are seen immediately across all channels due to a Google Firebase Realtime Database back-end.

Based on the idea that 10,000 monkeys with 10,000 typewriters would eventually produce the works of Shakespeare, the app allows users to view and modify their favorite {wordblock}s and take snapshots of results to share with friends, use for creative inspiration, or for iterative editing with a group.

10kT is currently in open beta on the Google Play Store for testing and development.

Please contact nilsberndt@gmail.com with any questions or comments related to the app or concept.
